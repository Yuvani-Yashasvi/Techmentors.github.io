Tech Mentors one year Anniverssary celebrate

Success is measured by effort, social impact, the size of one’s dream, the value which is being created and spread out and the appreciation that is given in return. And for the last 12 months, Web Academy has proven to be a real life-changer which incorporates big dreams and brave decisions, makes a difference, delivers value and results from our determination and collaborative work. Established one year ago on the 20th of auguest, 2020, we are now celebrating not only Web Academy’s one-year anniversary, but one year full of achievements and success stories.

Tech Mentors one year Anniverssary celebrate at main building tech mentors on 20 Auguest with strict adherence to health guidelines. The primary thematic of the event was the promotion and facilitation of a Online Education. The Ceremony began with the hoisting of the national flag and paying respect to the national anthem. Thereafter symbolic silence was observed to commemorate the national heroes, founders and later employees of BEIN University. This was followed by the Chairman Tech mentors, Prof.Rashen Kodituwakku addressing the audience comprising of tech mentors staff.

Administrative Assistant

Job Overview:
The Administrative Assistant provides administrative assistance to the Director of Academics to ensure effective organization, program implementation, and communication to our students, instructors, and staff.

Responsibilities:
Coordinate administrative requests, including:
Support academic operations in all regions
Support new program implementations
Research educational training options
Enroll students in Canvas
Design and distribute program curriculum in collaboration with the Director of Academics
Coordinate instructors for training
Schedule students for training
Data entry
Records Management
Ensure that projects are done correctly and in a timely manner.
Attend and take minutes at meetings.
Assist in maintaining student records and databases.
Assist in maintaining online curriculum (i.e., Canvas).
Maintain confidentiality in processing sensitive information.
Contribute to fostering a sense of community within the organization.
Other duties as assigned.

Qualifications:
Bachelor's Degree (recent graduate preferred)
Proficient in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel) and capacity to learn other technical skills.
Experience, either as a student or educator, working with Canvas or another web-based learning management system preferred.
Organizational and interpersonal skills sufficient to manage staff and students.
Must be detail-oriented, flexible, and adept at multi-tasking.
Strong desire to understand and support Workforce Opportunity Services' mission.

Location:
Remote (Work from Home)
